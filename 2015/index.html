Weight Loss Competition 2015!

The goal of this weight loss competition is NOT to lose a lot of weight quickly. Instead, the focus of this competition is to promote healthy weight loss, and for competitors to maintain a healthy weight. Competitors will NOT be penalized for meeting their goals. Every person has a chance to win.

Rules/Guidelines:

  1. The competition lasts 4 months. (January 20th - May 18th)
  2. To enter the competition, there is an entry fee of $50.
  3. You set your own weight loss goal.
  4. Every Monday, competitors are weighed. Points are awarded as follows:
    • Competitors who lose weight during the week receive 1 point.
    • Competitors who DO NOT lose weight receive 0 points.
    • Competitors who do not lose weight, but meet and/or are maintaining their set goal receive 1 point.
  5. As a week by week incentive, each competitor who wins a point can inflict the week’s punishment on the competitors who did not win a point.
    • Each week’s punishment will be push-ups equal to the current week of the competition multiplied by 2. (Week one is 2 push-ups, week two is 4 push-ups, etc.)
    • Each winning competitor can demand punishment from each losing competitor, but only once during the week. The timing of the punishment is up to the winning competitor.
    • Losing competitors must perform the punishment IMMEDIATELY.
  6. If you meet and maintain your set goal at the end of the competition, your $50 entry fee is returned to you.
  7. If you fail to meet your set goal, your $50 is awarded to the winner(s).
  8. The winner is the person(s) with the most points.
  9. A special award is also awarded to the person who goes above and beyond their goal. This award is determined by a vote at the end of the competition from all competitors.
