Weight Loss Competition 2016!

The goal of this weight loss competition is NOT to lose a lot of weight quickly. Instead, the focus of this competition is to promote healthy weight loss, and for competitors to maintain a healthy weight. Competitors will NOT be penalized for meeting their goals. Every person has a chance to win.

Rules/Guidelines:

  1. The competition lasts 11 weeks. (June 16 - Sept. 1st)
  2. To enter the competition, there is an entry fee of $20.
  3. You set your own weight loss goal.
  4. Every Monday, competitors are weighed. Points are awarded as follows:
    • At every weigh in, if a competitors has lost weight he/she will be awarded 1 point.
    • Competitors will not be awarded a point if he/she hasn't loss weight.
    • Competitors who do not lose weight, but meet and/or are maintaining their set goal receive 1 point.
  5. The winner is the person(s) with the most points.
